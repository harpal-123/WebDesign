About College

With a view to promote Quality in Higher Education in the rural area of Bhusawal Tahasil of Jalgaon District (earlier known as East Khandesh).

Tapti Education Society has been promoted by social workers and Philanthropists way back in 1958. The Bhusawal Arts, Science and P. O. Nahata Commere College, Bhusawal was then established by Tapti Education Society in 1963,the first college in Bhusawal Tahasil.

The college is committed to "Committed to Excellence in Higher Education to Empower Youth - with Modern Views, Foresight and Global Competency along with Social Commitment - for Nation Building" .The college is affiliated to North Maharashtra University, Jalgaon.

Our college got accredited in 2001, 2008 and in 2014 consecatively and achived ****, A(CGPA 3.28) and A(CGPA 3.30) respectively; thus being the first college in North Maharashtra University Jalgaon jurisdiction to achive hattrick of 'A' Grade.
